When a guest calls room service at The Evanstonian, the room-service manager takes down the order.
She then submits an order ticket to the kitchen.
She also gives an order to the sommelier.
Finally , she assigns the order to the waiter.
While the kitchen prepares the food, the sommelier fetchs wine from the cellar and prepares other alcoholic beverages and the waiter readies a cart. Cart arrangement includes putting of a tablecloth on the cart, gathering  silverware and preparation of nonalcoholic drinks.
Once the food, wine, and cart are ready, the waiter delivers it to the guest’s room.
Then waiter returns to the room-service station.
If he has no other order to prepare or deliver, the waiter debits the guest’s account.
Else he may wait to do the billing.
